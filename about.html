A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Integrity Clean does professional commercial cleaning in Wellington. The business was started in 1991. Aaron takes care of the Wellington work. We clean small offices right through to large manufacturing companies.

How clean is Integrity Clean?

When your work involves both toilets and cafeterias you especially need to have and apply an excellent understanding of hygiene. This not only helps reduce the spread of germs andsickness in the work place but also, can make you and your employees feel as comfortable at work, as you are at home. Integrity Clean has systems in place to ensure your health is not compromised!

Keeping it Simple

As the Owner/Operator, Aaron will help eliminate any of the problems you may have had with other big companies in the past. Our formula is simple, "make it clean and tidy!"

About Aaron

Aaron started Integrity Clean as a form of part time employment, to enable him to be more involved in study and other pursuits. He soon became aware that there was a real need for honest commercial cleaners, who could use their initiative and consistently leave buildings clean and tidy. Aaron soon employed some of his family then eventually other staff.
