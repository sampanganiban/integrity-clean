Our Services

A Typical Night

We call security to say we are on site - if required. We clean toilets, showers, restock consumables, wash our hands then clean kitchens, do dishes, remove rubbish, replace bin liners, dust, vacuum, clean window and mop. Then last but by no means least we set the alarms, turn off your lights and lock up.

HYGIENE AND CLEANING PRODUCTS

We use BIODEGRADABLE products and the correct amounts of dilution, which is often overlooked in caring for the environment.

SEPARATE EQUIPMENT is reserved for the cleaning of toilet areas and rubber gloves are worn.

WE DO NOT CARRY TOILET BRUSHES AROUND. This means there is no chance of contaminated water dripping onto surfaces and other areas, outside the toilet area. All toilets we clean must have their own toilet brushes.

WINDOW CLEANING

Internal windows and partitions are cleaned by us. We also clean low level external windows (no more than four stories). For safety reasons when doing high level windows we prefer to use a professional window cleaning company. We are happy to organise this for you.

CAR PARK SWEEPING

We have a variety of sweeping machines and clean internal multi- storey car parks, outdoor car parks and street frontage areas.

TRADITIONAL FLOOR WAXING SERVICE

Our top end traditional floor waxing service is for hardwood floors. Often hardwood floors are the centre of attention in a reception area or even in a home. As it's a natural product (bees wax), it requires a little more maintenance than other polishes. However, if you want the great smell that comes from a freshly waxed floor, and that warmth and swirly sheen, then it's worth the extra cost.
